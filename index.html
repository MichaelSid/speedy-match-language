How to Play

A Second Language word will appear. Select the correct English translation from four options. Correct answers earn 10 points; incorrect answers deduct 5 seconds. Game time is based on your word list: 3 seconds per pair, with a minimum of 30 seconds (e.g., 20 pairs = 60 seconds).

Enter Word Pairs

Copy two columns (English and Second Language) from a spreadsheet, or type pairs manually. Tabs or commas between words are automatically detected. You can also use one pair per line (e.g., English,Second Language).
